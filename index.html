Randomized Signoffs

This is fun if you want to spit out little quirky signoffs while composing messages.

  1. Drag this link to your bookmarks bar: Signoff.
  2. While composing a message in a text input, click the bookmarklet to signoff wherever your cursor. Repeatedly clicking will randomly select signoffs.

The signoffs mostly came from Wikipedia.
